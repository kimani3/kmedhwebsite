Welcome to The Nairobi Hospital

Our Services

Welcome to The Nairobi Hospital, your premier destination for world-class healthcare services in Kenya. With a rich legacy of excellence spanning decades, we pride ourselves on delivering exceptional medical care with compassion, expertise, and innovation.

At The Nairobi Hospital, we offer a comprehensive range of services designed to meet the diverse healthcare needs of our patients. From preventive care and diagnostics to advanced treatments and surgical procedures, our dedicated team of healthcare professionals is committed to providing you and your loved ones with the highest standard of care at every step of your journey.

Theatre Services

The Nairobi Hospital's theatre services offer state-of-the-art facilities and expert medical professionals dedicated to providing high-quality surgical care. Our theatres are equipped with advanced technology and adhere to strict safety and hygiene standards to ensure optimal patient outcomes.

From routine procedures to complex surgeries, our experienced surgical team delivers personalized care with compassion and precision. Trust The Nairobi Hospital for excellence in surgical services, where your health and well-being are our top priorities.

Neonatal Intensive Care Unit

Welcome to The Nairobi Hospital Neonatal Care unit, where we provide specialized medical care for newborns in need of intensive treatment and support. At our state-of-the-art facility, we are dedicated to ensuring the health and well-being of premature infants and newborns facing medical challenges.

Our Neonatal Care unit is staffed by a team of highly skilled and compassionate healthcare professionals, including neonatologists, pediatric nurses, respiratory therapists, and other specialists. With years of experience and expertise in neonatal care, our team is committed to delivering the highest standard of medical care to our youngest patients and their families.

Equipped with advanced medical technology and equipment, our unit offers a wide range of specialized services tailored to meet the unique needs of each newborn. From respiratory support and nutritional therapy to monitoring and treatment of medical conditions, we provide comprehensive care to promote the healthy development and growth of premature and critically ill infants.

At The Nairobi Hospital Neonatal Care unit, we understand the importance of family-centered care. We strive to create a supportive and nurturing environment where families are actively involved in their baby's care and decision-making process.

Our team works closely with parents to provide education, emotional support, and resources to help them navigate the challenges of caring for a newborn in the neonatal intensive care unit (NICU).

Whether your baby requires short-term intensive care or ongoing support, you can trust The Nairobi Hospital Neonatal Care unit to provide compassionate and personalized care every step of the way. We are dedicated to helping every newborn thrive and reach their full potential.

For more information about our Neonatal Care unit and the services we offer, please contact us or visit our facility. Thank you for considering The Nairobi Hospital for your newborn's care.

Diagnostic Services

Welcome to The Nairobi Hospital's cutting-edge diagnostic services, where we prioritize accuracy, efficiency, and patient care. Our state-of-the-art facilities and expert medical professionals ensure that you receive the highest quality diagnostic testing and interpretation.

At The Nairobi Hospital, we offer a comprehensive range of diagnostic services to meet the diverse needs of our patients. From routine screenings to advanced imaging and laboratory tests, we provide the tools necessary for early detection, diagnosis, and monitoring of various medical conditions.

Our diagnostic imaging department is equipped with advanced technology, including MRI, CT scan, ultrasound, and digital X-ray machines, allowing for precise and detailed visualization of internal structures. With our experienced radiologists and technicians, you can trust that your imaging studies will be interpreted accurately and promptly.

In addition to imaging services, our on-site laboratory is fully equipped with the latest instrumentation for a wide range of tests, including blood work, urine analysis, microbiology, and pathology. Our dedicated team of laboratory professionals ensures fast and accurate results, enabling timely diagnosis and treatment.

At The Nairobi Hospital, we understand the importance of timely and accurate diagnosis in guiding patient care. That's why we strive to deliver exceptional diagnostic services that are accessible, efficient, and tailored to meet your individual needs. Your health and well-being are our top priorities, and we are committed to providing you with the highest standard of care at every step of your diagnostic journey.

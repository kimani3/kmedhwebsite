About Us

Kenneth Matiba Hospital has been serving the community with excellence in healthcare for many years. Our team of dedicated professionals is committed to providing compassionate, high-quality medical services.

Our mission is to promote health and wellness through comprehensive, patient-centered care in a modern facility designed for comfort and healing.

The hospital features specialized units including an urgent care center for critical conditions and a separate outpatient unit for walk-in patients. Our facilities include modern treatment rooms, comfortable patient wards, and state-of-the-art medical equipment.

Every aspect of our hospital is designed with patient dignity and privacy in mind, from our private rooms to our specialized care units. We combine medical expertise with compassionate care to deliver exceptional patient experiences.

Our Services

Kenneth Matiba Hospital offers a comprehensive range of healthcare services designed to meet the diverse needs of our community. From preventive care to advanced treatments, our dedicated team provides compassionate, high-quality medical care.

Theatre Services

Our ophthalmic theatre features state-of-the-art facilities and specialized eye surgeons dedicated to providing exceptional ophthalmic care. Equipped with advanced technology for eye procedures and adhering to strict safety standards, our theatre supports everything from routine cataract surgeries to complex retinal procedures. Our experienced ophthalmic surgical team delivers personalized vision care with precision and compassion.

Diagnostic Services

"Our diagnostic services support our specialized care in dentistry and casualty services. Our on-site laboratory provides essential testing for blood work and urinalysis, ensuring prompt and accurate results for your treatment. While we focus exclusively on dental, laboratory, and emergency care, our qualified technicians deliver reliable diagnostics to meet your needs..

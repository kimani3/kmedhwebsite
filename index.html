Welcome to Kenneth Matiba Hospital

The Kenneth Matiba Eye and Dental Hospital, located in Kimorori/Wempa, Muranga South, Kenya, is a Ministry of Health primary care hospital fully operational as of 2021.

The hospital was named after veteran politician Kenneth Matiba and was dedicated to him in 2016. It provides specialized eye and dental care services and has treated thousands of patients since its commissioning. In April 2025, the hospital moved to a new facility in Kenol town as part of a state-of-the-art health facility construction project. The relocation aims to enhance the hospital's capacity to serve more patients in need of specialized care.

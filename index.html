About Kenneth Matiba Hospital

We are a premier healthcare facility specializing in comprehensive eye and dental care under the Ministry of Health. Our team of highly qualified specialists, state-of-the-art equipment, and patient-centered approach ensure you receive the highest quality care.

With years of experience and thousands of successful procedures, we've become the trusted choice for families seeking exceptional ophthalmology and dental services in a comfortable, modern environment.
